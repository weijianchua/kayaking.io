PULL		 Kayaking is a sport that almost everyone can do. It's simple to learn, affordable, and can be done alone or with friends. With a kayak, you can explore the world of water without restrictions, and it's one of the easiest way to broaden your horizons and perspectives about the world. It's a peaceful way to visit the local rivers while taking in the landscape and fauna.

To get started, do join the One Star Kayaking Courses, where you'll get hands-on experience while you learn and love the sport. At the start of the training, participants will be evaluated on their water confidence - put on your Personal Floatation Device and pass a brief swim test! Eventually, you'll hone your skill and graduate with a certificate.
